#CupCakeShop Project:


This is a project from our fourth week of semester 2. The whole project is uploaded and running on "Ubuntu Droplet" with SSH, MySQL & Tomcat8 services at DigitalOcean. It is a small web-shop for ordering a cupcakes. Commonly it is separated like this: 1.Log in form that opens a session, 2.Shop page where you are making the order(you chose the bottom&topping as you like it), 3.Pay the order with your balance , 4.Visit your profile page, 5.Enjoy your cupcakes!
